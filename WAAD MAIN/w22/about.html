About PICT

SCTR's Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a premier engineering college in India. It is well known for its strong academics, excellent placement record, and a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Vision

To be a leading technical institute known for excellence in education and research.

Mission

Impart high-quality technical education, encourage research, and foster industry collaboration.

History & Founding

Established in 1983 by the Society for Computer Technology and Research (SCTR), PICT has been a pioneer in technical education in India. Over four decades, we have grown into a premier institution known worldwide for academic excellence.

Key Achievements

  • AICTE approved and NBA accredited
  • Consistently ranked among top engineering colleges in India
  • 100% placement record for graduates
  • Global partnerships with international universities
  • Active innovation and startup incubation center
